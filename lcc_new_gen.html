Life Cycle Calculation

  • Bridges

    Large steel bridges are normally designed for an expected lifetime of 100 years In general therefore a number of maintenance and refurbishment operations will take place during the lifetime of the structure. The life cycle calculation program enables optimalization over the lifetime based on the knowledge of today. Because of the future uncertainty, the program offers simulation of all parameters.

  • Storage Tanks

    Large steel tanks are generally situated in agressive atmospheres often it is C5-industrial. Tanks also require maintenance and total refurbishment after certain periods. In such cases the life cycle calculation program can assist taking optimal decisions about new construction coating systems and maintenance plans. Life cycle calculations and simulations can assist decision makers to make good decisions.

  • Infrastructure

    Infrastructure projects are often complicated structure comprising steel structures that are difficult to access for maintenance and refurbishment. It is therefore important that a lifecycle view is taken over the lifetime to minimize cost of staging surface preparation, paint application and access for these operations, and to optimize economy and reduce VOC over the lifetime. Maintenance cost is a major issue.

Life Cycle Calculation program

The Life Cycle Calculation program (LCC) takes input from the project such as surface area, design life, interest rate, inflation rate, time to maintennce, time to refurbishment and other details.
There is two versions of the life cycle calculation program the lite version that gives an overview and simulation options in one screen and the full version with much more functionality, such as storage and retrieval of projects, printing and calculation modules for the cost of the various operations.
